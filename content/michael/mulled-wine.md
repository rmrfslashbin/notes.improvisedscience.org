## Ingredients

- 750ml dry red wine (1 bottle)
- 60ml (¼ cup) brandy or orange liqueur
- 1 orange, sliced into rounds
- 8 whole cloves
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 2 star anise
- 2–4 tbsp sugar, honey, or maple syrup, to taste

### Optional Garnishes

- Citrus slices (orange, lemon, and/or lime)
- Extra cinnamon sticks and star anise

## Instructions

1. **Combine.** Add wine, brandy, orange slices, cloves, cinnamon sticks, star anise, and 2 tbsp sweetener to a large saucepan. Stir briefly.
2. **Simmer.** Heat over medium-high until the wine just barely begins to simmer — do not let it boil, or you'll cook off the alcohol. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for at least 15 minutes or up to 3 hours.
3. **Strain.** Pour through a fine-mesh strainer to remove the orange slices and whole spices. Taste and stir in additional sweetener if needed.
4. **Serve.** Ladle into heatproof mugs and add garnishes if desired.

> **Less bitter:** Peel the orange before slicing if you prefer a gentler flavour.
>
> **Cheesecloth:** Bundle the orange slices and spices in cheesecloth before adding to the wine. Lift out the bundle instead of straining when ready to serve.
>
> **Slow cooker:** Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker and heat on low for 1–3 hours.
