This is Mom's famous pie.

## Special Equipment

-   9" Deep Dish Pie Plate
-   One recipe [Pâte Brisée]({{< ref "/recipes/pate-brisee" >}})

## Preheat Oven

-   Preheat oven to 375°F

## Ingredients

-   1 19 oz can Cherry Pie Filling
-   1 14 oz can Whole Cranberry Sauce
-   60ml (¼ cup) Sugar
-   3 tablespoons Quick Cook Tapioca
-   1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
-   ¼ teaspoon Cinnamon
-   2 tablespoons Butter

## Preparation

1.  Combine all ingredients, except butter, let stand 15 minutes.
2.  Pour in in a crust lined 9" deep dish pie plate.
3.  Cut butter into small pieces and scatter over mixture.
4.  Place a top layer of crust over mixture and seal.
5.  Sprinkle sugar over top of pie.
6.  Cut holes to allow air escape and expansion.

## Bake

1.  Bake about 1 hour
