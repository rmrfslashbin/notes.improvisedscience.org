This is mom's classic apple cobbler recipe.

## Info

-   Recipe by: Cheryl Sigler
-   Preparation time: 30 minutes
-   Cook time: 2 ½ hours

## Equipment

-   9x13x2" Baking dish
-   Two recipes [Pâte Brisée]({{< ref "/recipes/pate-brisee" >}})

## Preheat Oven

-   Preheat oven to 425°F

## Ingredients

-   4.5kg (10 lbs) Granny Smith Apples: Peeled, Cored, Sliced
-   480ml (2 cups) Sugar
-   120ml (½ cup) All Purpose Flour
-   2 tablespoons Cinnamon
-   1 tablespoon Nutmeg
-   1 tablespoon Allspice
-   120ml (½ cup) Butter
-   4 Pie Crusts [Pâte Brisée]({{< ref "/recipes/pate-brisee" >}})

## Preparation

1.  Line a deep 9x13x2" baking dish with two pie crusts.
2.  Combine all ingredients (adding spices to taste) and pour onto pie
    crusts.
3.  Cut butter into 8 pieces; drop pieces over mixture.
4.  Top mixture with the remaining pie crusts.
5.  Cut vents in top of crust
6.  Top with additional sugar & spice mixture

## Bake

1.  Cover with heavy foil and bake 2 ½ hours.
2.  Remove foil and bake another 30 minutes.
