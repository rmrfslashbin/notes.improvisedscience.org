Classic Chex Mix recipe.

## Special Equipment

9x13 roasting pan

## Preheat Oven

-   Preheat oven to 250°F.

## Ingredients

-   6 tablespoons Butter
-   2 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
-   1 ½ teaspoons Lowery's Season Salt
-   ¾ teaspoon Garlic Powder
-   ½ teaspoon Onion Powder
-   720ml (3 cups) Chex Corn Cereal
-   720ml (3 cups) Chex Rice Cereal
-   720ml (3 cups) Chex Wheat Cereal
-   240ml (1 cup) Mixed Nuts
-   240ml (1 cup) Pretzels
-   240ml (1 cup) Bagel Chips

## Preparation

1.  Melt butter in roasting pan in oven
2.  Stir in seasonings
3.  Gradually stir in remaining ingredents until evenly coated

## Bake

1.  Bake 1 hour, stiring every 15 minutes

## Storage

-   Store in air tight container
