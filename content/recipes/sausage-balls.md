A great sausage ball recipe. Meat substitutes can be in place of pork sausage meat.

https://www.cookscountry.com/recipes/7168-sausage-balls

## Equipment

-   Parchment paper
-   Baking sheet

## Preheat Oven

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F
2.  Adjust oven rack to middle position

## Ingredients

-   213 all-purpose flour
-   1 teaspoon baking powder
-   ¼ teaspoon salt
-   ¼ teaspoon pepper
-   1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
-   28 grams (1oz) unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
-   310 grams (11oz) bulk breakfast sausage or veggie sausage
-   130 grams (5oz) sharp cheddar cheese, shredded (1 cup)
-   180ml (¾ cup) buttermilk

## Preparation

### Combine

1.  Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
2.  In a food processor, pulse together just until combined (about three
    pulses):
    -   flour
    -   baking powder
    -   salt
    -   pepper
    -   cayenne
3.  Add **butter** and pulse until mixture resembles coarse meal, about
    12 pulses.
4.  Add **sausage** and **cheddar** and pulse until combined, about 8
    pulses.
5.  Transfer mixture to bowl and stir in **buttermilk** until combined.

### Form into Balls

1.  Working with wet hands, roll 1 tablespoon dough at a time into
    1%frac14;-inch balls and place, evenly spaced, on baking sheet.
2.  Bake until golden brown, **20 to 22 minutes**, rotating baking sheet
    halfway through baking.
3.  Transfer baking sheet to wire rack and let balls cool for **5
    minutes**.
4.  Serve warm.

Combine all, roll into balls and bake.
