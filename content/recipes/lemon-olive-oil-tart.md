## Special Equipment

-   Fine-mesh strainer
-   9" Tart pan
-   Rimmed baking sheet
-   Rasp-style zester
-   Medium saucepan
-   Wire cooling rack

## Preheat Oven

Preheat oven to 350°F

## Ingredients

### Crust

-   215g (8oz) all-purpose flour
-   65g (2oz) sugar
-   2.5 grams table salt
-   120ml extra-virgin olive oil
-   30ml (2 tbsp) water

### Filling

-   200g (7oz) sugar
-   15g (1oz) all-purpose flour
-   1 gram table salt
-   3 large eggs plus 1 large yolks
-   15 ml grated lemon zest
-   120ml lemon juice (about three lemons)
-   60ml extra-virgin olive oil
-   Clip on candy thermometer or instant-read thermometer

## Method

### Crust

1.  Adjust oven rack to **middle position** and preheat oven to
    **350°F**.
2.  Whisk together:
    -   flour
    -   sugar
    -   salt
3.  Add **olive oil** and **water**; stir until a uniform dough forms.
4.  Using your hands, crumble **three-quarters** of the **dough** over
    the bottom of the **tart pan**.
5.  Using your fingers, press the dough to an even thickness over the
    bottom of the pan.
6.  Crumble **remaining dough** and press into **fluted sides** of pan.
7.  Place tart pan on a **rimmed baking sheet** and then bake until
    crust is **deep golden brown**' and **firm to touch**, about **30 to
    25 minutes**, **rotating half-way** through baking.
8.  Remove from oven.

### Filling

**10 minutes before crust is finished baking**...

1.  Whisk together in a **medium saucepan**:
    -   sugar
    -   flour
    -   salt
2.  Whisk in **eggs** and **yolks** until no streaks of egg remain.
3.  Whisk in **lemon zest** and **lemon juice**.
4.  Cook over **medium-low** heat, whisking constantly and scraping
    corners of the saucepan until mixture registers **160°F**, about **5
    to 8 minutes**.

### Combine, Strain, and Bake

1.  Off heat, whisk in **olive oil** until incorporated.
2.  Strain curd though a fine-mesh strainer set over a bowl.
3.  Pour strained curd into warm tart shell.
4.  Bake until filling is set and barely jiggles when pan is shaken,
    about **8 to 12 minutes**.

### Cool

1.  Remove tart from oven and cool on a wire rack at least **2 hours**.
2.  Move tart to refrigerator and chill until firm, about **1 hour**.
3.  Remove outer tart-pan ring.
4.  Slide a spatula between crust and tart pan bottom and carefully
    slide tart onto a serving platter.
