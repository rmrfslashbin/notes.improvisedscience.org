This recipe is modified from the source to cut out extra fat and boost flavor.

## Source

https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1017797-pumpkin-bread-with-brown-butter-and-bourbon

## Change Log

-   Swapped unsweetened applesauce for oil
-   Swapped extra bourbon for water
-   Swapped dark brown sugar for light
-   Increased cinnamon and ginger slightly
-   Chose 4 cardamon pods instead of measured amount

## Special Equipment

-   2 9" loaf pans
-   Parchment Paper

## Pre-heat Oven

-   Pre-heat over to 350°F
-   Move rack to center position

## Ingredients

-   113g (4oz) unsalted butter (1 stick)
-   60ml (¼ cup) bourbon (plus extra; see notes below)
-   1 tablespoon vanilla
-   420ml (1¾ cups) pumpkin purée ([Roast Pumpkin]({{< ref "/recipes/pumpkin-pie#roast-pumpkin" >}}))
-   4 eggs
-   120ml (½ cup) unsweetened applesauce
-   256g (9oz) all-purpose flour
-   120g (4oz) whole wheat flour
-   350g (13oz) dark brown sugar
-   1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
-   5 grams fine sea salt
-   10 grams ground cinnamon
-   2 grams ground ginger
-   4 cardamom pods; seeds ground

## Preparation

### Prep Pans

1.  Grease the insides of two 8-inch loaf pans with butter
2.  Line with parchment paper; butter the paper
3.  Dust with flour

### Brown Butter

1.  In a large skillet, melt **butter** over medium-high heat.
2.  Reduce heat to medium and cook until the frothy white milk solids
    sink to the bottom of the pan and turn a fragrant, nutty brown, **5
    to 7 minutes**.
3.  Brown butter can burn quickly, so watch it carefully.
4.  Brown butter is almost ready when the frantic sound of bubbling
    begins to die down.

### Wets

1.  In a glass liquid measuring cup, combine **bourbon** and
    **vanilla**.
2.  Add additional **bourbon** until you reach the 2/3 cup mark.
3.  In a large bowl, whisk together bourbon mixture, **pumpkin purée**,
    **eggs** and **applesauce**.
4.  With a spatula, scrape all the brown butter from the skillet into
    the pumpkin mixture and stir to combine.

### Drys

-   In another large bowl, whisk together:
    -   all-purpose flour
    -   whole wheat flour
    -   brown sugar
    -   baking soda
    -   salt
    -   cinnamon
    -   ginger
    -   cardamom

### Combine

1.  Pour liquid ingredients into dry ingredients and stir to combine.

### Bake

1.  Divide batter between the two greased loaf pans. Use a scale to
    ensure equal amounts are in both pans.
2.  Place them on a rimmed baking sheet and transfer to oven.
3.  Bake for **50 to 60 minutes** or until a tester or toothpick
    inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
4.  Allow bread to cool completely before removing from pan.
