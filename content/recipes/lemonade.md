Simple lemonade. Serve with a shot of vodka (chili pepper vodka is
great!), tequila, or rum.

## Ingredients

-   420ml (1¾ cups) granulated sugar
-   360ml (1½ cups) fresh squeezed lemon juice (\~ 8 to 10 medium lemons)
-   240ml (1 cup) + 1680ml (7 cups) cold water

## Preparation

### Simple Syrup

1.  In a small sauce pan, combine the first 240ml (1 cup) of water with sugar.
2.  Stir occasionally over medium-high heat until mixture comes to a
    full boil.
3.  Remove from heat and cool to room temperature.

### Squeeze Lemons

1.  Render 360ml (1½ cups) of lemon juice.
2.  Strain.
3.  Chill until ready to use.

### Combine

1.  Combine simple syrup, lemon juice, and remaining 1680ml (7 cups) cold water.
2.  Chill until ready to serve.
