These blueberry muffins are light, delicious, and delightful. Use fresh
blueberries for full flavor and opt for muffin wrappers as opposed to
non-stick spray for even lighter tasting muffins.

## Info

-   Source: Cooks County Magazine

## Special Equipment

-   12 cup muffin tin
-   Muffin wrappers **-or-**
-   Non-stick cooking spray

## Preheat Oven

1.  Preheat oven to 375°F
2.  Adjust rack to middle of oven

## Ingredients

-   480ml (2 cups) All Purpose Flour
-   ½ teaspoon Salt
-   2 Eggs
-   180ml (¾ cup) Light Brown Sugar
-   4 tablespoons Unsalted Butter- melted, cooled
-   180ml (¾ cup) Non-fat Buttermilk
-   1 teaspoon Lemon Zest
-   2 teaspoons Lemon Juice
-   1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract
-   240ml (1 cup) Fresh Blueberries

## Preparation

1.  Line a 12 cup muffin tin with muffin wrappers **-or-**
2.  Spray a 12 cup muffin tin with non-stick cooking spray

<!-- -->

3.  Whisk together, in a large bowl
    -   flour
    -   baking powder
    -   baking soda
    -   salt

<!-- -->

4.  In a separate bowl, whisk together until throughly combined
    -   eggs
    -   brown sugar

<!-- -->

5.  Gradually whisk in, one by one, until well blended
    -   melted butter
    -   buttermilk
    -   lemon zest
    -   lemon juice
    -   vanilla

<!-- -->

6.  Fold into dry mixture
7.  Toss blueberries with 1 tablespoon flour and fold into batter

<!-- -->

8.  Distribute batter into the muffin tin.

## Bake

1.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes, rotating pan halfway through baking, until
    light golden brown and toothpick inserted into center comes out
    clean.
