## Ingredients

-   480ml (2 cups) grated boiled potatoes
-   240ml (1 cup) all-purpose flour
-   120ml (½ cup) semolina
-   ½ teaspoon salt
-   1 egg

## Preparation

### Boil Potatoes

1.  Boil 6 large potatoes in a pot of water until tender.
2.  Peel and grate to make 480ml (2 cups).

### Prepare Dough

While preparing dough, boil another pot of salted water in which to cook
the dumplings.

1.  With your hands, in a large bowl, mix together:
    -   Potatoes
    -   Flour
    -   Semolina
    -   Salt
2.  Make a well in the center and add an egg.
    1.  Mix the egg with the potato mixture to form a dough.
    2.  Cut the dough into four sections.
    3.  Roll the dough into four logs.
    4.  Drop into a pot of boiling water.

## Cook

Boil for 15 or 20 minutes. Remove from water and cool. When cool slice
into sections ½ inch thick.
