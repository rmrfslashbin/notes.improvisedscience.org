## Equipment

-   2 small bowls
-   1 baking sheet large enough to hold salmon

## Prehet Oven

1.  Adjust oven rack to the center position.
2.  Preheat oven to 250° F.

## Ingredients

-   1 tablespoon packed brown sugar
-   1 teaspoon salt + ½ teaspoon salt
-   ½ teaspoon pepper
-   One 1.1kg (2.5 lbs) center-cut salmon fillet, about 1.5" thick, skin-on
-   60ml (¼ cup) extra-virgin olive oil
-   2 tablespoons sliced fresh dill
-   2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
-   1.5 tablespoons lemon juice

## Preparation

### Roast Salmon

1.  Combine **brown sugar**, **1 tsp salt**, **pepper** in a small bowl.
2.  Place **salmon**, skin side down, on baking sheet.
3.  Sprinkle sugar/salt mixture over **salmon**.
4.  Roast salmon about **55 to 60** minutes or until center registers
    **125°F**.

### Combine Oil

1.  While the salmon is roasting, combine **olive oil**, **dill**, **½
    tsp salt**, **lemon zest**, and **lemon juice** in small bowl.
2.  Once salmon is cooked, remove from oven and pour oil mixture evenly
    atop salmon.
3.  Let sit **5 minutes**
