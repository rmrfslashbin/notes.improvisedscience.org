For this recipe, substitute low-fat buttermilk in place of heavy cream
for a lower fat yet creamy mouth-feel. Also, make your own crust. It's
so easy...

## Source

-   [Mushroom and Gruyère Quiche](http://www.wholefoodsmarket.com/recipe/mushroom-and-gruy%C3%A8re-quiche)

## Special Equipment

-   Rimmed baking sheet

## Preheat Oven

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F

## Prerequisite Recipes

-   Half a recipe of [Pâte Brisée]({{< ref "/recipes/pate-brisee" >}})

## Ingredients

-   113g (¼ lb) cremini mushrooms, cut into quarters or eighths, depending
    on size
-   113g (¼ lb) portobello mushroom caps, diced
-   113g (¼ lb) shiitake mushrooms, stems removed and discarded, cut into
    quarters
-   ½ medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
-   1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
-   ½ teaspoon salt
-   ½ teaspoon black pepper
-   120ml (½ cup) low-fat buttermilk
-   2 eggs
-   1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
-   112g (4oz) gruyère cheese, shredded (about 1 ½ cups)
-   2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme

## Preparation

### Roast Mushrooms and Onions

1.  In a large bowl, toss:
    -   Cut mushrooms
    -   Onion
    -   Olive oil
    -   Salt
    -   Pepper
2.  Place on a rimmed baking sheet in a single layer.
3.  Roast for about 15 minutes, or until cooked and firm.
4.  Remove from oven and set aside.

===Bake Pie Shell==

1.  Prick pie shell all over with a fork.
2.  Prebake shell for 10 to 15 minutes, until lightly browned.
3.  **Reduce oven temperature to 350°F.**

### Combine Eggs

1.  In a large bowl, whisk together:
    -   Buttermilk
    -   Eggs
    -   Thyme
    -   Salt
    -   Pepper.

### Combine Mushrooms

1.  In another large bowl, combine:
    -   Roasted mushrooms & onion
    -   Gruyere
    -   Thyme
2.  Spread mushroom mixture in the bottom of the pie shell in an even
    layer.

### Bake Quiche

1.  Pour cream-egg mixture over the mushroom mixture, ensuring that all
    of the mushroom mixture is covered.
2.  Bake for 45 to 55 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center
    of the quiche comes out clean.
3.  Serve warm or at room temperature.
