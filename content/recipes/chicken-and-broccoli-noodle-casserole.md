For this recipe, you will need 340g (12oz) (or 2 good sized
chicken breasts) of cooked, shredded chicken. The chicken can be cooked
ahead of time by boiling two chicken breasts in a pot of seasoned
chicken broth for about 1 hour, or until the meat begins to fall apart.

## Source

-   http://www.skinnytaste.com/2012/02/chicken-and-broccoli-noodle-casserole.html

## Ingredients

-   170g (6oz) noodles
-   2 tsp oil
-   4 cloves garlic, sliced thin
-   340g (12oz) fresh broccoli florets, chopped
-   14g (½oz) butter
-   1 medium shallot, minced
-   15g (½oz) all purpose flour
-   ¼ tsp cyanne pepper
-   420ml (1¾ cups) fat free chicken broth
-   240ml (1 cup) 1% milk
-   340g (12oz) cooked shredded chicken breast
-   115g (4oz) aged cheddar cheese
-   cooking spray
-   25g (1oz) shredded Parmesan cheese
-   30g (1oz) breadcrumbs

## Equipment

-   9 x 12 casserole dish
-   Large skillet
-   Large pot

## Pre-heat

-   Preheat the oven to 375°.

## Preparation

1.  If needed, cook chicken breasts.

In a large pot:

2.  Cook noodles in salted water until al dente, or slightly undercooked
    by 2 minutes. Set aside.
3.  Wash pot for later use.

Next, heat oil in a large skillet.

4.  Add garlic and cook on medium heat until golden, about 1 minute.
5.  Add the broccoli and a little salt, sauté and cover the broccoli for
    about 3 minutes on medium heat until the broccoli begins to soften.
    Set aside.
6.  Lightly spray a 9 x 12 casserole dish with cooking spray.

In a large pot:

7.  Melt butter over medium-low heat
8.  Add the shallot and cook until soft, 2-3 minutes.
9.  Add the flour and a pinch of salt and stir well, cooking an
    additional 2-3 minutes on medium-low heat.
10. Slowly whisk in the chicken broth until well combined over medium
    heat
11. Add the milk and bring to a boil.
12. Simmer on medium heat, mixing occasionally until mixture thickens
    (about 6-7 minutes).

Remove from heat:

13. Add cheddar cheese
14. Cyanne pepper
15. 1 tablespoon of the parmesan cheese
16. Mix well until the cheese melts.

Combine all:

-   Chicken
-   Broccoli
-   Sauce

Pour into casserole dish:

17. Top with Parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs.
18. Bake 20 to 25 minutes.
