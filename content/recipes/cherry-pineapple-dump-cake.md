This is an old "easy recipe" which is deliciously deadly. Eat with care.
The "Cherry Pineapple" rendition is just one way this cake can be
prepared; the basic recipe can be altered to use any flavor of cake mix
with any variety of canned fruits.

## Special Equipment

-   9x13" Cake Pan

## Preheat Oven

-   Preheat oven to 350°F.

## Ingredients

-   1 can cherry pie filling
-   1 can crush pineapple, in natural juice
-   120ml (½ cup) chilled butter, cut into half tablespoon pieces
-   1 packaged yellow cake mix

## Preparation

1.  Grease a 9x13" cake pan.
2.  Dump cherry pie filling into cake pan.
3.  Dump crushed pineapple (including juice) into cake pan.
4.  Stir to combine cherries and pineapples; spread evenly.
5.  Dump yellow cake mix atop fruits; spread evenly.
6.  Evenly distribute butter pieces over top of cake.

## Bake

1.  Bake 45 minutes or until cake mix is golden brown.

## Variations

-   Strawberry pie filling with chocolate cake mix
