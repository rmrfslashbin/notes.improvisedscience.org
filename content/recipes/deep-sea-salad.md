Patricia Ku's famous "Deep Sea Salad"

## Special Equipment

-   8x8x2" cake pan
-   Blender

## Ingredients

-   1 package Orange Jello
-   960ml (4 cups) Water, boiling
-   600ml (2½ cups) Mini Marshmallows
-   240ml (1 cup) Heavy Cream
-   224g (8oz) Cream Cheese
-   120ml (½ cup) Mayonnaise
-   1 package Lime Jello

## Preparation

1.  Combine orange jello with 2 cups boiling water.
2.  Stir 2 minutes; pour into blender.
3.  Add into blender; process until smooth
    -   mini marshmallows
    -   heavy cream
    -   cream cheese
    -   mayonnaise
4.  Pour into an 8x8 cake pan.
5.  Refrigerate overnight.

1.  Combine lime jello with 2 cups boiling water, stir 2 minutes.
2.  Cool to room temperature.
3.  Pour over refrigerated mixture.
4.  Refrigerate until set.
