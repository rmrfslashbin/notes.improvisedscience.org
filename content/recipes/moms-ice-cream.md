Mom's mythical ice cream; not for those on a diet or fearful of
egg-borne bacteria.

## Special Equipment

-   Ice Cream Freezer

## Ingredients

-   8 to 12 eggs (higher egg content yields a more rich tasting final
    product)
-   24 fl. oz. (2 large cans) of evaporated milk
-   720ml (3 cups) sugar
-   Pinch of table salt
-   2 teaspoons vanilla
-   Whole Milk, to fill freezer

## Preparation

1.  In a large bowl, combine eggs & sugar.
2.  Add vanilla and evaporated milk, stir well.
3.  Pour into ice cream freezer.
4.  Fill freezer with whole milk to the "full line", stirring mixture
    while adding milk.
5.  Add in additions as desired: can of chocolate syrup, strawberries,
    pineapple, etc.

## Freeze

1.  Freeze ice cream per manufacturer's directions.
