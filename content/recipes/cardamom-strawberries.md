## Ingredients

-   1 quart fresh strawberries, hulled, quartered, or sliced
-   120ml (½ cup) water
-   Seeds of 15 cardamom pods, lightly ground
-   1 tablespoon granulated sugar
-   1 tablespoon honey
-   1 teaspoon molasses
-   2 tablespoons bourbon

## Method

### Syrup

Into a small saucepan, add:

-   Water
-   Cardamom seeds
-   Sugar
-   Honey
-   Molasses

Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until reduced by 75%; stir
occasionally.

1.  Add bourbon and stir until the alcohol has evaporated (30 to 60
    seconds).
2.  Let cool to room temperature.

### Add to strawberries

1.  Places hulled and sliced strawberries in a non-reactive bowl.
2.  Pour syrup over strawberries.
3.  Lightly stir.

Let set for at least one hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
