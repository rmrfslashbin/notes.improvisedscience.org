Super easy and delicious. This recipe can be varied in a number of ways
as desired.

## Source

-   http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/grilled-pastrami-style-salmon

## Ingredients

-   1 (680g / 1 ½-pound) center-cut salmon fillet
-   1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
-   1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
-   1 teaspoon kosher salt
-   ¼ to ½ teaspoon ground allspice
-   1 teaspoon garlic powder
-   1 teaspoon ground ginger
-   1 teaspoon ground coriander
-   ½ teaspoon olive oil
-   Cooking spray

## Preparation

1.  Prepare grill.
2.  Combine first 7 ingredients.
3.  Place salmon fillet, skin side down, on a cutting board or work
    surface; brush evenly with olive oil.
4.  Sprinkle spice mixture evenly over salmon; gently rub mixture into
    fish.
5.  Cover lightly with plastic wrap, and chill 15 minutes.
6.  Place fish, skin side down, on a grill rack coated with cooking
    spray; grill 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with
    a fork.
