The title says it all. This recipe pairs well with [Potato Dumplings]({{< ref "/recipes/potato-dumplings" >}})

## Ingredients

-   1 medium yellow onion, chopped
-   1 medium purple onion, chopped
-   448g (16oz) white button mushrooms, chopped
-   896g (32oz) sauerkraut, drained
-   Broth, as needed (vegetable, chicken, beef, etc. Your choice)
-   Salt
-   Olive Oil

## Preparation

1.  In a large skillet, combine onions and mushrooms.
2.  Add some salt.
3.  Add enough olive oil to lightly coat and prevent sticking.
4.  Cover and cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until
    mushrooms and onions soften.
5.  Uncover and continue over low heat, stirring frequently, until
    mushrooms turn deep brown and liquid has cooked off.
6.  Add sauerkraut; stir to combine.
7.  Cover; stir frequently to prevent burning; add broth as needed to
    maintain moisture.
8.  Cook until sauerkraut turns deep brown.

## Serve

You may serve as is, or compliment with as side dish.
