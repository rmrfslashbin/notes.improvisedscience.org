## Source

Milk Street, May 2019

## Special Equipment

-   Large dutch oven
-   12" skillet
-   A paper-towel-lined plate
-   Kitchen towel

## Ingredients

-   1200ml (5 cups) water
-   4 cloves garlic, smashed and peeled
-   4 bay leaves
-   2 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
-   ½ teaspoons ground allspice
-   1 tablespoon salt
-   ¼ teaspoon salt
-   1 teaspoon ground black Pepper
-   240ml (1 cup) brown lentils, rinsed and drained
-   240ml (1 cup) basmati rice, rinsed and drained
-   80ml (⅓ cup) extra-virgin olive oil
-   2 medium yellow onions, halved and thinly sliced
-   1 bunch scallions, thinly sliced

## Preparation

### Water and Spices

1.  In a large Dutch oven over **medium-high**, combine:
    -   1200ml (5 cups) water
    -   garlic
    -   bay leaves
    -   cumin
    -   allspice
    -   salt
    -   pepper

### Add Lentils

1.  Bring to a boil, then add **lentils**.
2.  Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally and
    adjusting heat to maintain a **simmer** until lentils are softened
    but still quite firm at the center- about **10 minutes**.

### Add Rice

1.  Stir in rice, return to a **simmer**.
2.  Cover, reduce heat to **medium-low** and cook until the liquid is
    absorbed and the lentils and rice are tender- about **25 minutes**.

### Meanwhile, cook onions

1.  In a 12" skillet, heat **oil** until shimmering.
2.  Add **onions** and cook, stirring occasionally at first and then
    more frequently once browning begins, until the onions are deeply
    caramelized and crisped- about **10 to 15 minutes**.
3.  Using a slotted spoon, transfer the onions to a paper-towel-lined
    plate, spread thin and evenly.
4.  Sprinkle with **¼ teaspoon salt** and set aside.

### Finish

1.  Once the lentils and rice are tender, remove from heat, uncover, lay
    a kitchen towel over the pot, and place the cover over the towel for
    **10 minutes**.
2.  Remove cover and towel, fluff with a fork.
3.  Discard bay leaves.
4.  Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed.
5.  Stir in half the **scallions**.
6.  Transfer to a serving bowl.
7.  Top with the **onions** and remaining **scallions**.
8.  Serve.
